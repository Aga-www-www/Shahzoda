"Why all of this?"

If what I did hurt you, I have no excuses for it. I simply want to say that I am sorry.

Forgiveness is not an obligation. But asking for forgiveness is my responsibility.

📖 And now, I have one small request from you...

Please read the book I gave you slowly and thoughtfully. I did not choose it randomly.

The lines I marked inside it are not meaningless. Sometimes a book can say the words that I could not say. Sometimes an author's words can express the feelings that I could not explain.

That is why, please do not just read those marked lines.

Reflect on them.

Maybe those lines will tell you the words I could not say. Maybe you will understand not me, but my silence.

I will be waiting eagerly for you to read the marked pages.

Thank you. 🤍
