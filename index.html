If what I did hurt you, I have no excuses for it. I can only say that I am truly sorry.

Forgiveness is not an obligation. But asking for forgiveness is my responsibility.

Please read the book I gave you slowly and carefully. I did not choose it randomly.

The lines I marked inside it are not meaningless. Sometimes a book can say the words that I could not express. Sometimes an author's words can describe the feelings that I could not explain.

So please, don't just read those lines.

Reflect.

Maybe those words will tell you what I could not say. Maybe you will understand not me, but my silence.

I will be waiting for you to read the marked pages.
