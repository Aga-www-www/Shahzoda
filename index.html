Don't rush...

Take a moment to be alone with yourself.
Calm your breathing.
Collect your thoughts.

Read every line not only with your eyes,
but with your heart.

🕊 First of all...

If I have ever hurt you or made you feel bad, I sincerely apologize.

Thank you for coming this far. Maybe you are wondering: "Why all of this?"

The truth is, I had two ways to reach you. One was with honesty and kindness. The other was trying to get your attention in the wrong way.

Unfortunately, I chose the second one. But now I understand something important: attention cannot be forced. It is earned through respect, patience and sincerity.

If my actions hurt you, I have no excuses. I can only say: I am truly sorry.

"Forgiveness is not an obligation, but asking for forgiveness is my responsibility."

📖 One small request...

Please read the book I gave you slowly. I did not choose it by accident.

The lines I marked inside it have meaning. Sometimes a book can say the words that we cannot express ourselves.

So please don't just read those pages. Reflect on them.

Taammul.

Maybe those words will tell you what I could not say. Maybe you will understand not only my words, but also my silence.

I will be waiting for you to read those marked pages.

Thank you. 🤍
